Text · Tom Mullaney and Jason A. Heppler

Original

下馬飲君酒， 問君何所之？ 君言不得意， 歸臥南山陲。
但去莫復問， 白雲無盡時。

Translation

Dismount the horse and have some wine / I ask, to where are you going? / You say you cannot guess / And will rest at the foot of the Southern Mountain / You merely depart and will not return, is what I hear / A white cloud drifts without end.
